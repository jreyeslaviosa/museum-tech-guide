Datapath is a UK manufacturer of video wall controllers, multi-output graphics cards, and video capture hardware. Their products are found in control rooms, visitor attractions, digital signage installations, and broadcast environments where a computer needs to drive a large tiled display array with precision.

## Key Products

**FX4 — Video Wall Controller Card**
The FX4 is Datapath's flagship PCIe graphics card with four independent DisplayPort outputs. It is designed specifically for video wall applications — driving a 2×2 or 1×4 display arrangement from a single card with pixel-perfect alignment. Multiple FX4 cards can be installed in one workstation to scale up to larger walls.

**WallControl 10**
Datapath's software for managing content layout across a video wall. Windows-based; drag windows and sources across the display array, create preset layouts, and manage multiple inputs.

**Vision Capture Cards**
Datapath also makes the Vision range of capture cards — PCI/PCIe cards for ingesting HDMI, SDI, and other video sources into a Windows workstation. Used in installations where live video feeds need to be displayed or recorded alongside generated content.

## Datapath vs Media Servers

Datapath FX4 is a display controller card, not a media server. It extends the desktop across multiple screens with precision geometry correction. For straightforward multi-screen video wall use without complex content management, it is simpler and more cost-effective than a full media server workflow.

## Use Cases

- Driving a 2×2 or 4×1 LCD video wall in a gallery or lobby from a single workstation
- Control room display walls showing multiple data feeds simultaneously
- Permanent installations where a simple, stable multi-output display is needed without media server complexity
