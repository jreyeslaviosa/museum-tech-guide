Green Hippo (now part of Christie Digital) produces the Hippotizer media server range — professional hardware/software systems widely used in live events, concerts, theatre, and large-scale installations. Hippotizer is known for its reliability under touring conditions and its deep integration with professional lighting consoles via CITP and Art-Net.

## Hippotizer Range

- **Karst+:** Entry-level; 2 outputs; suited for smaller installations and one-off events
- **Amba+:** Mid-range; 4 outputs; the workhorse for most touring and installation work
- **Boreal+:** High-end; 8 outputs; large-scale productions and permanent venues
- **Tierra+:** Flagship; 12+ outputs; stadium and arena-scale shows

## What Makes It Different

Hippotizer's MediaManager library system makes managing large video libraries across multiple shows straightforward — assets sync across network nodes automatically. Its SHAPE engine handles real-time 3D geometry for warping and projection mapping.

It is particularly strong in environments where a lighting console operator (running GrandMA or ETC Ion) needs to trigger and control video cues from the lighting desk directly.

## Use Cases

- Live event and concert touring where the media server must run reliably for hundreds of consecutive shows
- Large museum or visitor attraction shows where content is cued from a lighting console
- Multi-output permanent installations with complex real-time content blending
- Combining live camera feeds with pre-rendered content in real time
