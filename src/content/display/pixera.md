Pixera is a professional media server platform by Austrian company AV Stumpfl, designed with permanent installations and complex multi-output shows in mind. It offers a clean node-based compositing engine, tight hardware integration with AV Stumpfl's Wings media servers, and a strong track record in museums, visitor attractions, and theme parks.

## What Makes It Different

Where Disguise dominates live touring, Pixera has carved out a strong position in permanent installations — partly due to its licensing model (no per-show fees), its stability for unattended long-term operation, and its compositing approach which suits gallery environments where content evolves over time.

Its timeline and compositing engine is powerful for shows that mix video, generative content, live feeds, and real-time data.

## Use Cases

- Permanent multi-projector and multi-screen gallery installations
- Visitor attraction shows requiring years of unattended daily operation
- Complex projection mapping on architectural surfaces with warping and edge-blending
- Shows combining pre-rendered video with real-time generative or data-driven content

## Museum Relevance

A strong candidate when specifying permanent large-scale installations where long-term reliability, low maintenance, and strong 3D mapping tools are priorities.
