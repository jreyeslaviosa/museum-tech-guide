LED panels (also called LED tiles or LED modules) are modular, self-emissive display panels that tile together to form large seamless video walls and sculptural displays. They are characterised by their pixel pitch — the distance in millimetres between pixels — which determines resolution and optimal viewing distance.

## Common Types

- **Fine pitch (≤ 2.5mm):** Indoor close-viewing walls, control rooms, high-resolution backdrops
- **Indoor (2.5–5mm):** Gallery walls, lobby displays, interactive floors
- **Outdoor (6mm+):** Building facades, outdoor signage, large venue installations

## Use Cases

- Permanent gallery feature walls delivering video art content
- Curved and shaped LED surfaces wrapped around sculptures or architecture
- Interactive LED floors using transparent or diffused panels
- Backdrop displays for live performances and events
