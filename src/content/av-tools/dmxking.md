DMXking manufactures compact, affordable Ethernet-to-pixel and Ethernet-to-DMX controllers. Their eDMX range are popular alternatives to Advatek for small to medium LED strip installations, offering ArtNet and sACN input with outputs for WS2811/WS2812 addressable strips and standard DMX512 fixtures.

## Key Products

- **eDMX1 PRO:** Single-universe ArtNet/sACN to DMX512 or pixel output; compact DIN-rail format for permanent installs
- **eDMX2 PRO:** Dual-universe version; useful for splitting a large LED run across two data lines
- **eDMX4 PRO:** Four-universe output; drives up to four independent pixel strips from a single controller
- **UltraDMX Micro:** Ultra-compact USB-to-DMX interface for laptop-based control

## DMXking vs Advatek PixLite

| | DMXking eDMX | Advatek PixLite |
|---|---|---|
| Universe count | 1–4 per unit | 4–16 per unit |
| Form factor | Compact / DIN-rail | Rack or wall-mount |
| Best for | Small–medium installs | Large pixel arrays |
| Price | Lower | Higher |

## Use Cases

- Driving individual LED strip runs in scenic or architectural lighting
- Permanent gallery installations requiring compact, unobtrusive controllers
- Prototyping and smaller-scale LED art pieces before scaling up
