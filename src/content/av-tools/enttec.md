Enttec is an Australian manufacturer of DMX interfaces and lighting control hardware widely used in creative technology. Their USB DMX interfaces (DMX USB Pro, Open DMX USB) are the most common way to connect a laptop or desktop computer directly to a DMX lighting rig.

## Key Products

- **DMX USB Pro:** Reliable two-universe USB-to-DMX512 interface; drivers supported by most lighting software
- **Open DMX USB:** Budget single-universe interface; open-source driver, no guaranteed timing
- **Pixie Driver:** IP-addressable LED pixel driver (receives ArtNet/sACN, outputs to WS2811/WS2812 strips)
- **STORM series:** Rack-mount ArtNet/sACN node for permanent installations

## Use Cases

- Connecting a laptop running QLC+ or QLab to a venue's DMX lighting rig
- Prototyping and testing DMX fixtures on the workbench before installation
- Permanent interface nodes in gallery spaces for network-to-DMX conversion
