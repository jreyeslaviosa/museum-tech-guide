GrandMA (manufactured by MA Lighting) is the industry-standard professional lighting console used in major theatre, concert touring, and permanent venue installations worldwide. The grandMA3 is the current generation; grandMA2 remains in widespread use. Both output DMX, ArtNet, and sACN natively.

## Use Cases

- Full show control for large-scale museum events and immersive experiences
- Permanent installation control in gallery spaces requiring professional-grade cuing
- Coordinating hundreds of intelligent lighting fixtures and LED systems across complex venues
- Timecode-triggered automated light shows synchronised with video and sound
