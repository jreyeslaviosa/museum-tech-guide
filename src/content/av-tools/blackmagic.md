Blackmagic Design makes video capture cards, converters, switchers, and cameras that are ubiquitous in professional AV, broadcast, and production environments. Their hardware is how video signals get into and out of computers — converting between HDMI, SDI, DisplayPort, and network video formats.

## Key Product Categories

**Capture Cards**
- **DeckLink series:** PCIe capture cards for desktop workstations; ingest SDI/HDMI into a computer for use in TouchDesigner, media servers, or recording
- **UltraStudio series:** Thunderbolt-connected capture devices; portable, used with laptops

**Converters & Mini Converters**
- Tiny boxes that convert between signal formats (HDMI↔SDI, analogue↔digital, embed/de-embed audio)
- Essential for connecting mismatched equipment throughout a venue signal chain

**Video Switchers**
- **ATEM series:** Hardware video switchers for live production; cut, mix, and key between multiple video sources in real time

**Cameras**
- **Pocket Cinema Camera (BMPCC):** High-quality camera used in creative installations requiring cinematic image quality

## Use Cases

- Capturing live camera feeds into TouchDesigner or a media server for real-time processing
- Converting projector signals between formats when equipment doesn't match
- Live switching between multiple video sources for events and presentations
- Getting clean HDMI capture from a game console or external device into a production system
