Stable Diffusion is an open-source AI image generation model that runs locally on your own hardware — no subscription, no API costs, no data sent to the cloud. ComfyUI is the most powerful open-source interface for Stable Diffusion, using a node-based workflow to build complex image generation pipelines. Together they form the backbone of most custom AI image work in creative technology.

## Why Local Matters

Running AI generation locally means full control: generate as many images as you want, train custom models on your own content, run in real time for interactive installations, and keep sensitive or proprietary visuals off cloud servers. A modern GPU (NVIDIA RTX 3080 or better) can generate 512×512 images in under a second.

## ComfyUI

ComfyUI's node graph interface will feel familiar to TouchDesigner and vvvv users. You connect models, samplers, prompts, and image processors into a pipeline. This makes it possible to build complex workflows: inpainting, style transfer, real-time video processing, and ControlNet-guided generation.

## Use Cases

- Generating concept art and visual development assets for exhibitions
- Real-time AI image generation in interactive installations (visitor inputs influence generated imagery)
- Style transfer and image processing pipelines for generative artwork
- Training custom models on museum collection images for collection-based AI experiences
