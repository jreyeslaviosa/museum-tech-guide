Runway is a creative AI platform focused on generative video and image tools, accessible through a web browser. Their Gen-3 Alpha model generates high-quality video from text or image prompts; their other tools handle video-to-video transformation, background removal, motion tracking, and more. Widely used by artists, filmmakers, and designers working with AI-generated content.

## Key Tools

- **Gen-3 Alpha (text/image to video):** Generate 5–10 second video clips from a text prompt or reference image
- **Act One:** Animate a character using motion capture from a simple video reference
- **Motion Brush:** Apply AI-generated motion to specific regions of a still image
- **Video-to-Video:** Transform existing footage using AI style transfer
- **Inpainting:** Remove or replace objects in video with AI-generated content

## Runway vs Stable Diffusion / ComfyUI

Runway is a subscription cloud service — faster to start, no hardware required, but per-credit costs add up quickly for high-volume generation. Stable Diffusion is local and free but requires technical setup. For one-off projects and quick exploration, Runway is faster; for ongoing production or interactive real-time use, local tools are more practical.

## Use Cases

- Generating atmospheric video content for exhibition backgrounds and installations
- Rapid visual concept development — turning a sketch into moving imagery for a pitch
- Creating AI-assisted artwork as a medium in its own right
- Video prototypes for digital commissions before full production
