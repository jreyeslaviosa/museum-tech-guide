ElevenLabs is the leading AI voice synthesis platform — it converts text to natural-sounding speech with a level of quality and emotional nuance that is difficult to distinguish from a human recording. For museum installations, it makes it possible to produce high-quality narration, character voices, and multilingual audio without recording sessions or voice actors.

## Key Capabilities

**Voice Cloning**
ElevenLabs can clone a voice from a short audio sample (a few minutes of recording). This is useful for maintaining a consistent narrator voice across content updates without re-booking voice talent, or for creating a museum-specific "character" voice.

**Voice Library**
A large library of pre-built voices with different accents, ages, and styles — usable immediately via API or the web interface.

**Multilingual TTS**
Supports 30+ languages with natural prosody, making multilingual audio guides significantly cheaper than commissioning human recordings in each language.

**API**
A simple REST API allows text-to-speech to be called programmatically — enabling dynamic narration in interactive installations that respond to visitor input.

## Use Cases

- Automated generation of multilingual audio guides from exhibit text
- Voice narration for interactive kiosk experiences that respond to visitor questions
- Dynamic character voices in narrative or theatrical installations
- Rapid prototyping of audio content before committing to human recording
